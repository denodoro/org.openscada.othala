The openSCADA™ Admin Client is split up in several perspectives. Each perspective focuses on one particular topic of the openSCADA system.

The following perspectives are available:

Client Perspective
The client perspective shows only minimal functionality. It acts as an entry point to all other functionality.
DA Perspective
The DA (DataAccess) perspective covers the OpenSCADA™ DA Interface. This is what was previously the the OpenSCADA™ Test Client.
Chart Perspective
The chart perspective replaces the HD (Histroical Data) perspective and provides chart views for HD and DA at the same time.
AE Perspective
The AE (Alarms&Events) prespective allows peeking into the alarms and events system.
